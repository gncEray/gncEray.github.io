Job Description:

We are seeking a talented and passionate writer to join our team and contribute to our art, book, and movie blog website. As a writer, you will be responsible for creating engaging and informative content related to various art forms, literature, and film. Your articles will cater to a diverse audience of art enthusiasts, book lovers, and movie buffs.

You can use application form to apply for becoming a writer, developer and/or designer.

Responsibilities:

  • Create engaging and well-researched articles, reviews, and features on art, books, and movies.
  • Stay updated on the latest trends, news, and releases in the art, book, and movie industry.
  • Develop unique and captivating story ideas.
  • Write in an engaging style that appeals to our target audience.
  • Collaborate with the editorial team to ensure content consistency.
  • Optimize content for search engines (SEO).
  • Proofread and edit your work for accuracy and clarity.
  • Adhere to deadlines and publishing schedules.
  • Engage with readers by responding to comments and fostering community.

Requirements:

  • Excellent writing skills with a strong command of English.
  • Passion and knowledge of art, literature, and movies.
  • Experience in writing blog posts or similar content.
  • Ability to conduct research from reliable sources.
  • Creativity in generating fresh content ideas.
  • Attention to detail and self-editing skills.
  • Familiarity with SEO principles.
  • Strong time management and communication skills.
  • Portfolio or writing samples related to art, books, or movies (optional).
